# Verifiable AI System Manual

Welcome to your private AI system.
This system is designed to be secure, private, and verifiable.

## Key Principles
1. **Trusted Sources**: Answers are grounded in documents you upload.
2. **Local Execution**: Models run on your hardware, not in the cloud.
3. **Verification**: Every action is logged and verifiable.

## Capabilities
- **Answer**: Ask questions about your documents.
- **Write**: Generate emails or reports based on your style data.
- **Generate**: Create code or images using local models.
